About Us

Learn about our journey, mission, and the team behind Car City - Delhi's premier destination for quality pre-owned cars.

Our Story

Car City was founded in 2010 by Rajesh Sharma, a passionate car enthusiast with over 15 years of experience in the automotive industry. What started as a small dealership with just 5 cars has now grown into one of Delhi's most trusted car resale businesses.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide quality pre-owned cars at fair prices with complete transparency. Over the years, we've expanded our services to include financing options, making car ownership more accessible to everyone.

Today, Car City is proud to serve thousands of satisfied customers across Delhi NCR, helping them find their perfect car with hassle-free financing solutions.

Our Mission & Values

Guided by our core principles, we strive to deliver exceptional service and value to our customers.

Quality Assurance

We thoroughly inspect and verify every vehicle before it enters our inventory to ensure the highest quality standards.

Transparency

We believe in complete transparency in all our dealings, providing honest information about every vehicle's history and condition.

Customer First

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We strive to exceed expectations and build lasting relationships.

Fair Pricing

We offer competitive pricing on all our vehicles and financing options, ensuring the best value for your money.
